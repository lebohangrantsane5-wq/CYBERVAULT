Founding Members

Founder & CEO

Miss Reitumetse Maunye

An electrical engineer with a passion for technology, Reitumetse encountered a frustrating reality while renovating her first home: smart home systems were either prohibitively expensive or unreliable DIY solutions with limited compatibility. Working from her garage during evenings and weekends, she developed a modular integration that unified multiple systems into one user-friendly network. What began as a personal project quickly gained interest from friends and neighbors seeking similar solutions.

Mission

Empowering the future of smart homes through advanced sound recognition technology, delivering smart, hands-free control that seamlessly connects people to their living spaces.

Vision

To build an inclusive future where smart home technology empowers everyone, regardless of ability, through sound-activated systems that make everyday living simpler, safer, and more accessible.

History

CyberVault has been at the forefront of smart home innovation since 2022, starting with a bold vision and ambitious goal driven by the continuous evolution of technology.
